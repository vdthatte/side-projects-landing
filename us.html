Chances are, the stories you love – the ones that have the greatest impact on you, your friends, and the people you and they care about; that mean something and make a difference, that make you all laugh and feel and think and see – started as somebody’s side project.

Side Projects is a production company dedicated to telling powerful stories that capture the honesty, vision and purpose of those passion projects – multigenerational and multicultural stories that resonate and resound because they speak straight from, and to, the heart.

Co-founded by Anthony Lawrence Hull and Demetrius Beckham, Side Projects is a collaborative venture with their extensive network of creative associates – actors, screenwriters, designers, editors, composers, etc. – of every age, gender and heritage, who’ve connected over their shared love of powerful storytelling and a desire to contribute to the cultural conversation in authentic and meaningful ways.
